Recycle Poster Series

2021 - Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

NEXT

This series of posters grabs the attention of viewers to persuade them to recycle and to use recycled goods. Each poster states the ratio between the CO2 emissions of a recycled material versus the same material not recycled. The textures try to highlight visual qualities of each material mentioned. Each poster has a secondary color palette, keeping them all cohesive with each other as a triad.
